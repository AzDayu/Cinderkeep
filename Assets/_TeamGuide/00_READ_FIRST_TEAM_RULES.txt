Cinderkeep - Read First

Purpose
- This project is shared by 9 team members.
- Each person chooses one character/class and works through that character's personal scene.
- Shared game work belongs in MainGame Muckhold first, inside Muckhold_Workspace, then moves into real systems when the team agrees.

Main Scenes
- Muckhold_Workspace: first scene and current MainGame Muckhold prototype.
- Main_Lobby: menu scene. Use this as the team entrance when you need character select or workspace links.
- SampleScene: character select scene.
- MainWorkspaceRoom_ForBuild: important integration room for completed work before commit, push, merge, and build checks.
- PersonalWorkspaceRoom: general personal workspace room.
- Archer_KwonSeonghyeok, Assassin_KimDonghyuk, Bard_CheonWooyoung, Blacksmith_ChoiJaeho, Healer_KangHeewon, Mage_JeongDongwon, Spellblade_KimMinseok, Summoner_JiJaewook, Tactician_Daniel, Tanker_KimSeonggwang: personal character scenes.

Team Rules
- Do not make large unrelated refactors.
- Do not edit another person's personal scene unless the owner agrees.
- Put shared prototype objects under WorkspaceRoot_Shared.
- Put personal experiment objects under your own WorkRoot_* object.
- Every room has a Main Camera with SceneCameraController.
- Every room canvas has UIManager linked to the scene camera.
- Name objects clearly so they are understandable in the Hierarchy.
- Prefer small components with one job over one large controller.
- Before changing shared code, check who depends on it.
- If a scene looks empty, confirm that you opened the correct scene from Assets/Scenes.

Unity Entry Flow
- Press Play to start in Muckhold_Workspace.
- Click Back To Lobby when you need Main_Lobby.
- Click !! Play Our Game (Prototype) to check the current game quickly.
- Click Team ID Cards to see class badges and member roles.
- Click Personal Work Rooms to enter a member's personal scene.
- Select a character card.
- Read Member, Class Badge, and Role Note.
- Click Open Personal Workspace to enter that character's scene.
